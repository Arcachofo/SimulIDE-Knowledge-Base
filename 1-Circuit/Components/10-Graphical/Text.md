## Description:

Add a text box to the circuit.

---

## Properties:

Property: (default value)

### Main:
- **Margin:** (5 Pixels)
   Space between text and border.
<br>
- **Border:** (1 Pixels)
   Width of border line.
<br>
- **Opacity:** (1)
   0 for transparent to 1 for 100% opaque.

### Text:
- **Font:** (Helvetica)
   Font family
<br>
- **Font Size:** (10 Pixels)
   Font size in pixels.
<br>
- **Fixed Width:** (yes)
   Characters have constant width or not.
<br>
- **Text Box:**
   Edit the text here.
