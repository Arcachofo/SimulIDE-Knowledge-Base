## Description:
Simple Clock source.

Turn On/Off with the letf buttton.

---

## Properties:
Property: (default value)

- **Voltage:** (5 V)
   Set output voltage.
<br>
- **Frequency:**  (1000 Hz)
   Set output frequency.
<br>
- **Always On:** (yes)
   Removes the button and keeps always active.
