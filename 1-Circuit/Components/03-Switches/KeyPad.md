Configurable keypad.

---

## Properties:
Property: (default value)

- **Rows:** (4) <br>
   Number of key rows. <br>

- **Columns:** (3) <br>
   Number of key columns. <br>

- **Key Labels:** (123456789*0\#) <br>
   List of characters corresponding to each key. <br>
   Left to right and top to bottom.
