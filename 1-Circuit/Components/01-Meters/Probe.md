Logic and voltage probe.<br>
Hover or connect to Wires or Pins to get voltage.

**As logic probe:**
- Orange = High State.
- Blue = Low State

---

## Properties:
Property: (default value)<br>

- **Show Voltage:** (yes)<br>
   Show voltage label. <br>

- **Threshold:** (2.5 V)<br>
   Digital threshold to determine High/Low states. <br>
