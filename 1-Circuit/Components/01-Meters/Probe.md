Logic and voltage probe.
Hover or connect to Wires or Pins to get voltage.

**As logic probe:**
- Orange = High State.
- Blue = Low State

---

## Properties:
Property: (default value)

- **Show Voltage:** (yes)<br>
   Show voltage value in circuit.

- **Threshold:** (2.5 V)<br>
   Digital threshold to determine High/Low states.
