## Description:

Group of resistors in a DIP configuration.

---

## Properties:

<br>
- **Size:** (8)
   Number of resistors.
<br>
- **Pullup:** (no)

- **Pullup Voltage:** (5 V)
   Voltage to connect the resistors.
