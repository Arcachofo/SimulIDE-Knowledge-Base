Simple capacitor.

Blinks if reactive step is too long.

---

## Properties:
Property: (default value)

- **Capacitance:** (10 uF)
<br>
- **Resistance:** (1e-6 Î©)
   Internal resistance.
<br>
- **Initial Voltage:** (0 V)
   Voltage at simulation start (initial charge).
<br>
- **Auto Step:** (0 steps)
   Resize Reactive step automatically.
   The value sets the number of sub-steps.
   0 to disable.
